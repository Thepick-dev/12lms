Rules

Picks

Each week pick one Premier League team to win. Each team may be picked exactly once; two pre-selected teams may be picked twice.

Players

Each pick also requires a player selection. Player goals = +3 points, assists = +2 points.

Scoring

Win vs Q1 = 6 • Win vs Q2 = 5 • Win vs Q3 = 4 • Win vs Q4 = 3. Draws award fewer points accordingly.

Leaderboard

Totals are calculated weekly. The player with the most points at season end wins.
